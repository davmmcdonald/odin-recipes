Holiday Spiced Latte

Who needs store-bought when you have a kitchen? With a baby and a dog, I don't have time for pumpkin puree, just need something to kick start my morning and put me in a good mood. I'm sure you'll share the feeling after you try this super-ample, super-fast, and super-cheap treat!

Prep Time: 5 mins
Total Time: 5 mins
Servings: 1
Yield: 1 cup

Ingredients
  • 2 tablespoons half-and-half
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar, or to taste
  • ¼ teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
  • 1 cup coffee
  • 1 tablespoon whipped cream topping, or to taste (Optional)
Directions
  1. Mix half-and-half, sugar, and pumpkin pie spice together in a coffee mug. Pour coffee into mug; stir to blend.
  2. Heat in microwave until hot, about 15 seconds. Top with whipped cream.
